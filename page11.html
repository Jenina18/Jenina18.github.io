Reason I created arts is because of inspirations for example weapons.
I like weapons so much from swords to guns sa sobrang nakaka hanag siya na-eenganyo ako gawin siya sa kahoy at i-ukit.
Sa drawing naman mostly is from anime i watched during childhood.
I was a big fan of ranma 1/2 cowboy bebop Detective Conan, City Hunter Daimos, and Voltes V.
What inspires me to paint is Bob Ross first time I watched him leave a big impression on me about painting and from that moment I started painting I also like Peter Paul Rubes, Van Gogh, Michellangelo, Da Vinci, Hokusai, and Miyamoto Musashi.
