Hi! Im Jomar G. Santos, Im 18 years old. My medium in art is the paper itself~Origami, a Japanese art of folding paper that turns paper into different models such as animals, flowers, insects and etc. I like it because it is unique in some point than drawing and painting, in addition to that, it is a 3D art piece that has lot of dimension. I love it because you can create any model by folding a (single) piece of paper. Origami taught me patience also, because it has lot of folds and should be precisely finished to achieve a good result. It all started, when I am being curious on how to fold a traditional origami crane when I was 12 years old.

Jomar Santos

Origami Artist, Handicrafts.
